About EasyMix

Reduce chicken feed costs while satisfying precise nutritional requirements.

Chicken feed is a major aspect in the poultry industry accounting to 60%-70% of costs. For local farmers it is less costly to formulate their own feed. EasyMix relieves the famrer of performing tedious feed calculations involved in manual methods of determining feed volumes and additives. Ensuring adequate nutritional requirements are satisfied in the chicken's diet.
